The Adventures of Ladybug

Ladybug is a fiesty five-month old kitten. She called Ladybug after her mother's admiration for naming animals after other animals. Come on an adventure with Ladybug, and experience a morning in her life!

Ladybug wakes up bright and early. She yawns and stretches, wiggling her little tail and paws. Does she;
curl up on Mother's chest and fall back asleep, or
wake up and start her day?
